Dribbling

Basics of Dribbling

Dribbling is one of the most improtant aspects of basektball. Learning how to dribble will help you get around the court confidently. When the ball is first in your hands you are in a position that is commonly known as the "Triple Threat." This means that you have three options to do with the ball.

  • Dribble
  • Pass
  • Shoot
But if you were to pick up your dribble, you are down to Passing and Shooting, which can limit your options. This is why having a solid foundation of dribbling can be important to improve as a player.

Types of Dribble Moves

There are so many different types of dribble moves players can have in their arsenal. But first it's best to get the basics down first. To start there will be pound dribbles, which is just dribbiling the ball with one hand.
